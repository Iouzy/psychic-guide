// FEATURES

Three sections, one daily flow

📋
Today

Intentions & reflection

Start each day by writing what you actually want to get done. Tick them off as you go and close the day with a short reflection. Honest, simple, yours alone.

⏱️
Focus · Pauta

Timed focus blocks

Run a focus block linked to one of your intentions. Pause, resume, add notes, and review everything on a scrollable timeline.

🌊
Tides · Marés

Habits with maritime tiers

Track habits on a monthly calendar. The longer your streak, the higher your tier — from Wave all the way up to Ocean. Use a Breath for guilt-free skips.

🔒 Built for privacy

Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no analytics, no third-party tracking — ever. Backup and restore as a single file whenever you want.

// INSTALL

Get Pauta on every device

🤖 Android

Download the latest .apk from the Releases page and open it to install. Your data persists across updates.

🍎 iPhone / iPad

Open the app in Safari → tap Share → tap Add to Home Screen. Works as a full-screen PWA.

💻 Desktop / Browser

Open the app in Chrome or Edge → click the install icon ⊕ in the address bar. Runs offline after the first load.
